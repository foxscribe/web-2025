About me

Hi! My name is Iaroslav, I'm 18. I'm a software developer, amateur artist, musician and writer. In my spare time I also explore video editing and motion graphics. I really like old devices and software and often use them as a working tool.

I am currently studying software engineering at university. Also, I finished music school in piano class in 2022 and graduated from a lyceum with physics and maths major in 2024. In addition to all that, I'm self-educating: developing my skills in writing stories and composing music, learning Objective-C and Pascal.

In the past, I have used various UNIX-like systems extensively, including GNU/Linux and BSD distributions. My favourite is Fedora, but I also love Debian and Red Hat Enterprise Linux. At this moment I'm actively using Mac OS X.

I don't play games often anymore, but I can name some of my favourites: Minecraft, OpenTTD and TIS-100. Maybe someday I will create my own game.

Goals

Also, I adore self-expression and creativity, so I'm learning digital art in particular and computer graphics as a whole, and maybe someday I'll become pretty good at it.
